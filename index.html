SHEET 01 / ABOUTPROFILE

Precision, then scale.

Douaa is a second-year Computer Science student at EPFL, currently holding a 5.94/6.00 cumulative average. Before EPFL, she ranked 6th nationally out of more than 300,000 students in the Moroccan Baccalaureate and graduated as her high school's valedictorian across every major, out of 200+ students.

Her projects follow one habit: rebuild the core mechanism from scratch before trusting it. That's meant training a transformer language model on campaign-rally transcripts, writing a parallelized Monte Carlo Tree Search agent to play the board game Azul, and hand-implementing decision trees and backpropagation during a graduate-level machine learning specialization.

The same instinct shows up off-screen — she has soldered the pressure sensors on a student rocket, and organizes sponsor meetings and cross-team coordination for EPFL's Rocket Team.
